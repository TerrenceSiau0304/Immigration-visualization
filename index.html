Capital of Malaysia

Kuala Lumpur is the capital and largest city of Malaysia. It serves as the country’s economic, cultural, and transportation hub.

Located in the Klang Valley, Kuala Lumpur is known for its modern skyline dominated by the iconic Petronas Twin Towers, vibrant street markets, diverse cuisine, and rich blend of Malay, Chinese, and Indian cultures.

It is one of Southeast Asia’s fastest-growing metropolitan areas, attracting tourists, businesses, and immigrants from around the world.

Ethnicity in Malaysia

Malaysia has 3 main races which are Malay, Chinese and Indian.

Each of these occupies ~70%, ~22% & ~7% respectively.
